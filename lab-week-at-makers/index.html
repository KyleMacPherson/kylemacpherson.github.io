Lab Week at Makers

05 October 2015

So it's Monday of lab week at Makers, the first day of the sixth week, and I am making my first blog post. What a guy.

Lab week is the equivalent of reading week at uni, except you actually do some work rather than drinking and playing FIFA.

My priorities for lab week:

  1. Set up a blog to keep track of my goals for lab week - Check!

  2. Read POODR - I have had this book recommended to me 10+ times over the last few weeks and I now finally have time to read it, so this is priority number one. This should also help with some of my other goals with lab week.

  3. Review my previous weekend challenges - I have a lot of feedback from various Makers coaches on my previous challenges and I will try to implement that this week, along with some jewels from the boss Sandy Mehtz (see 2).

  4. The Mihai Challenge - Mihai is, from what I can gather, a legendary Makers student/coach and this is his challenge. He set it for my friend who graduated about 6 months ago who has now persuaded (bullied) me into it. Basically it is the chitter challenge with a twist. More on this when I get to starting it.

If by some miracle I manage to finish all of this I will try to finish the awesome JavaScript video series I am watching on Udemy and have a quick run though the Rails course on Codecademy to prepare for week 7.

Good luck me!

Kyle MacPherson
